USDA Redesign

This project was an exploration of responsive web design, with a focus on heuristics and accessibility.

Project Overview

Background

Major agencies and initiatives that are household names. like SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program) and WIC (Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children), fall under the US Department of Agriculture’s umbrella. It has been called “The People’s Department”, and has impacted the lives of generations of Americans.


Problem

The USDA is a major resource for many user groups, but its confusing navigation and inflated pages make finding critical information difficult and slow. The information itself is focused on what the department has accomplished rather than what services the USDA can provide for the user.


Solution

Simplifying navigation and condensing pages to better address the specific user groups that the USDA serves allowed users to access the information they needed much more smoothly and efficiently.
Users saw a 140% increase in task completion and took 72 seconds less time to complete.
